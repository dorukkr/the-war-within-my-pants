About The War Within My Pants

Discover our World of Warcraft guild's story, values, and community.

Who We Are

The War Within My Pants is a dedicated World of Warcraft guild focused on raiding, Mythic+ dungeons, and building a strong community. We strive for excellence in all aspects of the game while maintaining a fun and inclusive environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for players who are passionate about WoW. Whether you're a seasoned raider or just starting your Mythic+ journey, you'll find a place here.

What We Offer

  • Regular raid schedules with competitive progression
  • Mythic+ coaching and group runs
  • Active Discord community for socializing and strategy
  • Friendly and supportive atmosphere

Join Us

If you're interested in joining our guild, please apply through our application form and join our Discord server. We look forward to raiding with you!
